MONTANA GAME CONFERENCE 2025

About

The Montana Game Conference (MGC) will take place on May 2nd-3rd, 2025, bringing together game developers, industry professionals, and enthusiasts for two days of collaboration, learning, and innovation. Attendees can look forward to engaging workshops, insightful panels, and keynote speakers covering game design, development, and the latest industry trends. The conference will also showcase indie games, providing developers a platform to share their work. Whether you're a seasoned pro or new to the gaming scene, MGC offers a unique opportunity to network, share ideas, and explore the growing gaming community in Montana
